Lasagna Recipe

Description

This classic lasagna recipe is made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel sauce, and plenty of melted cheese.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 24 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 jar (24 ounces) marinara sauce

Steps

  1. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions; drain and set aside.
  2. In a skillet, brown the ground beef, chopped onion, and minced garlic over medium heat until the meat is no longer pink; drain excess fat.
  3. In a bowl, combine ricotta cheese, 1 cup mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese, egg, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper.
  4. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  5. Spread a thin layer of marinara sauce in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  6. Place 3 lasagna noodles on top of the sauce.
  7. Spread half of the cheese mixture over the noodles.
  8. Spread half of the meat sauce over the cheese mixture.
  9. Repeat layers: noodles, cheese mixture, meat sauce.
  10. Top with the remaining 1 cup of mozzarella cheese.
  11. Cover with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  12. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until the lasagna is hot and bubbly.
  13. Let it stand for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
  14. Enjoy your delicious homemade lasagna!
