Krabby Patty Recipe

Description

The Krabby Patty is a beloved burger from the Krusty Krab in Bikini Bottom. This recipe brings the flavors of the sea to your kitchen with a tasty patty, secret sauce, and all the fixings.

Ingredients

  • 1 Krabby Patty bun
  • 1 Krabby Patty patty
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Pickles
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Secret Sauce (secret recipe)

Steps

  1. Heat the Krabby Patty patty on the grill until cooked to your desired level of doneness.
  2. Toast the Krabby Patty bun on the grill.
  3. Spread secret sauce on the bottom bun.
  4. Add lettuce, tomato, and pickles on top of the sauce.
  5. Place the cooked patty on the veggies.
  6. Add ketchup and mustard to taste.
  7. Top with the other half of the bun.
  8. Enjoy your Krabby Patty!
